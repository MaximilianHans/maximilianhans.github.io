Homepage of Maximilian Hans

I am a PhD student of Daniel Kasprowski at the University of Southampton. Before that, I was based in Bonn, working with Peter Teichner. My research interests lie primarily at the intersection of geometric topology and homotopy theory. This includes spaces of embeddings and diffeomorphisms, 4-manifolds, surgery theory, K- and L-Theory. I am currently working on a comparison between classical and modified surgery theory.

Here are some notes I have written covering various topics.

  • A Light Bulb Theorem for Multi-Disks.
  • The Kervaire Invariant and a non-smoothable Manifold.
  • The Pontrjagin-Thom Construction.
  • The Birman-Craggs Homomorphisms.
  • The Rokhlin Invariant and Homology Cobordism.
  • Symplectic Representation of the Mapping Class Group.
