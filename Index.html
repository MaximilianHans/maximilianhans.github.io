Research interests

My interests lie primarily in low-dimensional topology. Recently I have been interested in the interaction between homotopy theory and low-dimensional topology, specifically in the form of studying the homotopy type of embedding spaces and Goodwillie-Weiss embedding calculus. I am also interested in the relation between generalisations of the Kervaire-Milnor invariant, Whitney towers, and embedding calculus. Furthermore, the role of the Kervaire-Milnor invariant in the stable classification of 4-manifolds.
